Summary

The blood-soaked land of Kolar Gold
Fields (KGF) has a new overlord now -
Rocky, whose name strikes fear in the
heart of his foes. His allies look up
to Rocky as their savior, the government
sees him as a threat to law and order;
enemies are clamoring for revenge and
conspiring for his downfall.
